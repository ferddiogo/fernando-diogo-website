## The concept

Helix Tower started from the question: what if each apartment had exactly the amount of sunlight it needs? The parametric façade responds to each floor's solar orientation — denser brise-soleils to the west, more open to the east — generating a spiral texture that gives the project its name.

## The process

Geometry was defined in Rhino + Grasshopper, validated with solar incidence simulation (Ladybug Tools), and detailed in Revit for structural coordination.

- Structure: conventional post-tensioned
- Cladding: exposed ceramic brick in alternating bays
- Glazing: floor-to-ceiling with operable metallic brise-soleil

## The numbers

196 units across 28 floors. Typologies from 1 to 3 bedrooms, average area of 63 m². The parametric façade reduces thermal gain by ~18% compared to a conventional envelope on the same orientation.

## The starting point

A 1970s warehouse with 5.8 m ceilings and zenithal light through shed roofs. Preserved metal structure, stripped masonry, original polished concrete floor. The renovation was about **revealing**, not covering.

## The intervention

- Exposed metal mezzanine (catwalk + two private rooms)
- Long solid-wood benches (peroba), collective table at the center
- Suspended tubular lighting, complementing the natural zenithal light
- Interior glazing to zone the space without closing it visually

## The palette

Concrete gray + sand white + matte black + terracotta accents. No primary color, no gloss. Everything matte so the natural light is the star.

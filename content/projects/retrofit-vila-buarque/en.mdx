## The encounter

A 1938 building, art deco façade in precarious condition, 5 underused commercial floors and a deteriorated ground floor. Municipal heritage protection over the façade and roof. The client wanted 18 residential units without losing the character.

## The negotiation

The first part of the project was conversation: Conpresp (heritage council), neighbors, condominium. We built a shared brief: nothing new would appear on the main façade; air-conditioning consolidated to the rear; restored windows with discreet double glazing.

## The strategy

- **Ground floor** — restaurant + retail (keeping the original commercial character)
- **2nd–5th** — 16 residential units (50–110 m²)
- **6th** — 2 penthouses + common area + garden
- **Structure** — targeted carbon-fiber reinforcement on critical slabs
- **Systems** — fully new, in shafts created within the service stairs

## The schedule

14-month build. Expected delivery Q3/2026.

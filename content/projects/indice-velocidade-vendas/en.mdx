## The question

Which product, in which region, still has healthy absorption? For mid-size developers, the difference between launching at the right time and ending up with frozen inventory is a data-driven decision — not intuition.

## The data base

- Vertical launches in São Paulo capital, 2020–2024
- Sources: Embraesp, Geoimóvel, IPTU
- Manual enrichment: façade photo, construction status
- 98+ builders catalogued

## The method

Sales velocity (VSO) calculated by quarterly launch cohort, segmented by:

- Region (6 zones)
- Bedrooms (Studio to 5BR)
- Price band (5 ranges)
- Builder (top 30 + others)

## The delivery

Monthly Power BI panel with:

1. VSO heatmap by neighborhood
2. Builder × builder comparison
3. "Window" signal — product/region with VSO > 1.5σ above segment average
4. Automatic alert when a window opens or closes
